Bolsa Chica Rebrand

Visual identity for Bolsa Chica Land Trust

Role Designer
Timeline Ongoing
Tools Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

This rebrand for Bolsa Chica Land Trust modernizes the visual identity while honoring the wetlands and coastal habitat that define the organization. The updated system improves consistency across print, merchandise, social channels, and web touchpoints.

Logo

The logo suite includes primary and secondary variations designed to remain clear and recognizable across social icons, print collateral, signage, and environmental contexts. Each configuration preserves the core identity while adapting to different sizes, formats, and placement needs.

Color

The color palette draws from Bolsa Chica's wetlands, shoreline, and native vegetation. Earth-forward neutrals establish warmth and credibility, while accent tones introduce hierarchy and maintain accessible contrast for both digital and print communication.

Typography

The typography pairing balances personality with readability. Expressive display type establishes a distinct voice, while supporting styles provide structure and clarity for educational content, campaign messaging, and interface elements.

Merchandise / Physical Applications

Brand elements were explored through merchandise and physical goods to evaluate scale, placement, and legibility. The identity translates cleanly to items such as apparel, tote bags, and packaging while maintaining balance and visual clarity.

Digital / Social

The refreshed identity extends into social and web environments through structured typography, intentional color use, and cohesive component styling. These elements create a unified digital presence that supports storytelling and maintains brand continuity across platforms.
