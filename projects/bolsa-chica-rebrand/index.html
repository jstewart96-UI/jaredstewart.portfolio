Bolsa Chica Rebrand

This rebrand for Bolsa Chica Land Trust modernizes the visual identity while honoring the wetlands and coastal habitat that define the organization. The updated system improves consistency across print, merchandise, social channels, and web touchpoints.

Logo Variation

The logo suite includes primary and secondary lockups designed for flexibility across digital, print, and environmental applications. Each variation preserves recognizability while adapting to different sizes, formats, and context-specific constraints.

Color Palette

The core and secondary palettes draw directly from the wetlands, shoreline, and native vegetation. Colors were selected to create a natural, grounded tone while maintaining accessibility and contrast requirements for clear communication across mediums.

Typeface

The typography system pairs expressive display styles with highly legible supporting type. Together, they establish a confident voice and clear hierarchy for educational content, campaign messaging, and branded collateral.

Merchandise

Merchandise explorations test how the identity performs on wearable and physical goods, including tote bags and packaging. The visual system scales cleanly across formats while preserving legibility, balance, and brand consistency.

Social Media & Web

Social templates and website concepts translate the refreshed brand into a cohesive digital presence. Consistent typography, color usage, and component styling create continuity across channels while supporting engaging storytelling.
